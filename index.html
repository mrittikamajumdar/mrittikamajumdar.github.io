Mrittika Majumdar

Undergraduate at Rutgers Business School

Biography

Mrittika Majumdar is a current undergraduate student at Rutgers Business School-Newark where she is majoring in Finance, and minoring in Computer Science. She is also in the Honors College.

After graduating from high school in Kolkata, India, she moved to New Jersey, USA with her family and started her undergraduate career. She regards this relocation as life-changing and central to her identity. As a fierce believer in the American Dream, she strives to make the most use of opportunities both in and outside school.

Mrittika is passionate about empowering society through knowledge and education. She has volunteered in her community, working as a mentor for middle and high school students in after-school programs. She spends her time reading motivational books, watching TED talks and attending networking events. In the following semester, she is auditing a MBA course on Strategic Management.

Mrittika’s role model is Sheryl Sandberg. Upon graduation, she aspires to pursue her MBA from a top business school. Currently, she is looking for internship and networking opportunities in the corporate world.
